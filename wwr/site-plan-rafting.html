Overview

Purpose of Online Presence for Whitewater Rafting Company

The primary purpose of establishing an online presence is to increase brand visibility and streamline the booking process, ultimately driving more business and enhancing customer convenience.

Target Customers

Our main audience is adventurous 25-50 year-olds with disposable income, along with corporate teams seeking team-building activities.

Question: What would a 32-year-old parent like Sarah expect on our website?
Answer: Family-friendly rafting options, safety information, and pricing.

Question: What does Tim, an HR manager, need for his corporate team?
Answer: Group packages with team-building focus, group rates, and safety details.

Question: What unmet needs does Emily, a 29-year-old freelancer, have?
Answer: A mobile-responsive design, personal testimonials, and social media sharing options.
