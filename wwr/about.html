Whitewater Rafting Company

The Whitewater Rafting Company specializes in guided whitewater rafting adventures, offering a variety of tour packages designed to suit all skill levels.

History

The Whitewater Rafting Company has been delivering fun river adventures since 2018, turning novices into skilled rafters while showing off the beauty of our state's rivers. With a variety of rapids ranging from beginner to expert levels, we offer a range of experiences. Whether you are looking for a serene float down calm waters, or seeking the adrenaline rush that comes from navigating through Class V rapids, we have something for everyone.

Over the years, we have garnered numerous awards for safety and customer service, and have been featured in several travel and adventure magazines. As we continue to expand our offerings and explore new rivers, we remain steadfast in our original mission: to provide an unforgettable outdoor experience while showcasing the natural beauty and ecological diversity of our rivers.
